Who We Are

Idhika Foundation is working towards Empowerment of Women and Girl Child. We are trying to bring change in perspective of women and girl child issues and outlook towards elderly.

We want them to have better Quality of Life. Empowerment includes the action of spreading awareness through education, equipping and allowing women to make life determining decisions through different problems in society. We are trying to provide them a supportive atmosphere where they can try to achieve happiness, wellness and other basic needs like proper nutrition, cleanliness, medical care and skill development.

What We Do

Women are the pillars of society and when women are empowered the whole society is empowered and when a girl child is educated a whole generation sees change. Education holds the key to development, economic growth and social transformation.

We help them to acquire new skills and encourage them to study and develop personally, socially as well as economically. Income determines the quality if life of a person, by learning new skills one can improve standards of health and living. We help them to learn new skills, develop new interests and abilities, learn technical skills and communication.

We spread awareness about the need to look after the elderly, empower them by providing them supportive system and medical care, improve their mental health by Stress Management, improve their standards of health and living, provide them with expert guidance in Pain Management and try to full fill most of their basic needs and attend to their call of distress.
